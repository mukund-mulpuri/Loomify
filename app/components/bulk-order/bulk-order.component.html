Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our bulk pricing starts at 50 items per style. For orders below this threshold, please shop our regular retail collection.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 2-3 weeks after order confirmation. Rush options are available for an additional fee.

Can I order samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we offer sample ordering for bulk clients. Sample costs will be credited toward your final order.

Do you offer international shipping for bulk orders?

Yes, we ship bulk orders worldwide. International shipping costs and import duties will be included in your quote.
