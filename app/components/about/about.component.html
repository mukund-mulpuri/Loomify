The Loomify Journey

Loomify was founded in 2010 with a singular vision: to create exceptional clothing using the finest fabrics sourced from around the world. What began as a small boutique in New York has grown into a national brand with a reputation for quality and craftsmanship.

Our founder, Jane Smith, spent decades in the textile industry before establishing Loomify. Her expertise in fabric selection and sustainable manufacturing practices remains at the core of our business today.

Every Loomify garment is designed to be timeless, durable, and comfortable. We believe that clothing should be an investment, not a disposable commodity.

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our materials or craftsmanship. Each garment undergoes rigorous quality control before it reaches our customers.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible sourcing and production practices that minimize environmental impact and support ethical labor standards.

Innovation

We continuously explore new fabrics, techniques, and designs to bring you clothing that exceeds expectations in comfort and durability.

Community

We value the relationships we build with our customers, employees, and manufacturing partners. Together, we create something special.

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Loomify opens its first boutique in New York City, offering a small collection of premium cotton shirts and accessories.

2013

Expansion

After three successful years, Loomify expands to include a full range of clothing and opens a second location in Los Angeles.

2016

Sustainability Pledge

Loomify commits to sustainable manufacturing with our "Earth-First" initiative, transitioning to organic and recycled materials.

2019

National Reach

With stores in 10 major cities and a robust online presence, Loomify becomes a nationally recognized brand for quality fabrics.

2022

Innovation Hub

We launch the Loomify Innovation Center to research and develop the next generation of sustainable, high-performance fabrics.

Today

Looking Forward

With a focus on innovation and sustainability, Loomify continues to grow while staying true to our founding values.
